Branch area:

Kwaebibirem Rural Bank Limited which has seven (7) branches, namely, (Kwae Estate, Kade, Boadua, Pramkese, Asamankese and Oda). The Head Office is at Asuom which also serves as a branch.

The Bank was established in 1984 as a Rural Bank to offer banking services. The Bank had lived up to expectation of providing sustainable banking with excellent customer relationships for wealth creation and community development. The Bank is networked and one can operate within any of its numerous branches scattered around Birim Central, West Akyim Municipalities, Denkyembour and Kwaebibirem Districts. Kwaebibirem Rural Bank Limited makes it easier for you to access to financial services and products by coming to you whwerever you are.

We have quality staff who deliver excellent customer service and experience staff who will never fail you and ensure that your investment/money is safe. We have Field Officers and Mobile Bankers with good customer service to deliver at your door step.

Distribution channels


The Bank strategically locates its retail outlets in the fastest growing towns in Ghana to leverage its footprint to gain competitive advantage. It delivers its products and services through these principal channels: 7 Branches Network, Telephone Banking services via the Kwaebibirem Rural Bank Limited 24/7Contact Centre, Remote banking on Kwaebibirem Rural Bank Limited and Mobile Banking.
